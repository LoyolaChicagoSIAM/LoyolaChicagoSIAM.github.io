Join the Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (SIAM) at Loyola University Chicago for a "Math in Industry" panel designed to connect students with professionals in the field. Food and drinks will be provided for this networking and learning opportunity.

The panel features five distinguished speakers representing diverse sectors: Adriana Cortes (Arrow International, Inc.), Sam Hader (Light & Wonder), Gary Moon (Capital One), Sean Rogers (Bluberi), and Josef Sabuda (Fourier Data Consulting, LLC). These mathematicians and data scientists will share their experiences and insights into applying mathematical principles in industries ranging from finance and gaming to federal banking and consulting.
